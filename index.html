How was Antarctica discovered?

Antarctica was discovered on 27 January 1820, a Russian expedition led by Fabian Gottlieb von Bellingshausen and Mikhail Lazarev discovered an ice shelf at Princess Martha Coast that later became known as the Fimbul Ice Shelf.

The Antarctica was first governed by the UK which was the first of twelve countries to sign the Antarctic Treaty in 1959.
